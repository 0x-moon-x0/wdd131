Final Project Site Plan

Mary Made Stuff Overview

Site Name

I chose the name 'Mary Made Stuff' because it is the name of our family's small craft business, named after one of the main owners.

Site Purpose

The site contains general information about our business, a small catalog of items available for purchase, and form through which customers can inquire about one of our products or simply reach out for any other questions.

Scenarios

  • What does this store sell?
  • How do I request more information about an item?
